# JJK Absolute Validation Engine

This is a real-time, gesture-controlled domain expansion engine. 

## How to Use
1. **Activate:** Once the page loads, you will see an overlay. **Click the screen** to grant microphone and audio permissions.
2. **Camera:** When prompted by your browser, **click "Allow"** for camera access.
3. **Gestures:** Perform the following hand signals in front of your webcam:
    - **Sukuna:** Hold both hands together (clapping or holding) to trigger Sukuna.
    - **Gojo:** Form a crossed-finger gesture (index/middle overlapping, ring/pinky curled) to trigger Gojo.
    - **Mahoraga:** Hold one hand open (ensure fingers are not crossed/curled) to trigger Mahoraga.
4. **Reset:** Simply drop your hands out of the camera frame to reset to the IDLE state.

## Troubleshooting
- If the screen is black, ensure you have clicked the "Activate" button.
- If no gestures are detected, ensure you have sufficient lighting on your hands and face.
- Check the browser console (F12) if the engine fails to load assets.
